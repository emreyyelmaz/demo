About Us

The Hindu Society of Ottawa-Carleton (HSOC) is a non-religious, not-for-profit, non-political charity organization based in Ottawa, Canada established in 1992. Run by a group of elected volunteers, we support both Canadian and Indian charities with the support of generous donors. Majority of our activities are to support projects for charities in India.

get Informed

We focus on educating children, empowering women, and rural development. While the majority of our charitable activities are undertaken in India, we are also active locally and in the international arena.
